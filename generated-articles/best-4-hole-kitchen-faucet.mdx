### What is a 4 Hole Kitchen Faucet?

A 4 hole kitchen faucet system is designed for sinks that have four pre-drilled holes. These setups typically accommodate a main faucet spout, one or two handles for hot and cold water control, and an additional accessory such as a side sprayer, a soap dispenser, or sometimes even a filtered water tap. This configuration offers enhanced functionality and a classic, integrated look, making it a versatile choice for many kitchen layouts.
