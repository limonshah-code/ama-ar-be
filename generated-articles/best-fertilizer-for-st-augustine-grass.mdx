## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Fertilizer For St Augustine Grass

Choosing the right fertilizer for your St. Augustine lawn involves more than just picking a bag off the shelf. Understanding key factors will help you make an informed decision that leads to a healthier, greener lawn.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

Before you buy, identify what your lawn truly needs. Is it struggling with weeds, lacking vibrant color, or recovering from stress? St. Augustine grass has specific requirements, often thriving on a balanced NPK (Nitrogen-Phosphorus-Potassium) ratio with a higher nitrogen content for lush green growth, and often benefiting from iron supplements. Some fertilizers are general-purpose, while others are formulated for specific issues like weed control (weed and feed), rapid greening, or fall winterization. Ensure the product explicitly states compatibility with St. Augustine grass, especially varieties like Floratam, which can be sensitive to certain herbicides. Consider the time of year and your specific lawn goalsâ€”a spring fertilizer will differ from a fall one.
